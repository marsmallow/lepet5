About Us

Bistro Baron serves classic French Bistro favorites, highlighting the authentic yet homestyle French dishes. This is French comfort food, served in a casual and cozy decor.

With it’s charming interior, meticulously prepared food and a well thought-out drinks list, Bistro Baron is not only meeting the demand for authentic, high-quality dining experience, but it also infuses some French tradition and soul into Jakarta's bustling food scene.
